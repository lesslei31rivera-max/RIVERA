Using Technology to Spread Gods Light

FAITH AND TECHNOLOGY INTEGRATION

This page shows how technology can be used as a tool to spread positivity and faith. As a student, I see technology as more than just a tool for schoolwork or communication it is also a way for me to live out my faith. Through social media, online platforms, and digital projects, I can share positive messages, encourage others, and reflect Gods love in simple but meaningful ways. Even small actions online can become a way to spread light and hope.

Technology also helps me grow spiritually while balancing my responsibilities as a student. I use it to access Bible reflections, inspirational videos, and faith based content that strengthen my relationship with God, especially during stressful academic moments. It reminds me that faith can guide me even in a fast-paced, digital world.

By integrating faith and technology, I learn to use digital platforms responsibly and with purpose. As a student, I want my online presence to reflect my value showing kindness, respect, and integrity. In this way, technology becomes a channel not just for learning, but for spreading Gods light to others.
