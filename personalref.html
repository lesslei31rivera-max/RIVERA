Una ang Ginoo: Living with Faith and Integrity

PERSONAL REFLECTION

“Una ang Ginoo” is more than just a phrase for me, it is a guiding principle that shapes how I live my life. Putting God first means trusting Him in every decision I make, especially during moments of uncertainty, struggle, and doubt. There are times when life feels overwhelming, but remembering to place God at the center gives me strength and peace.

Living with faith teaches me to believe even when I cannot see the outcome. It reminds me that Gods plans are always greater than mine, and that patience and prayer are important in facing lifes challenges. When I choose to rely on God, I find comfort in knowing that I am never alone, no matter how difficult the situation is.

Integrity, for me it is my faith is reflected in my actions. It pushes me to do what is right even when it is not easy or when no one is watching. My faith calls me to be honest, responsible, and compassionate toward others. By living with integrity, I show respect not only to myself but also to God and the people around me.

Through this reflection, I realize that putting God first allows me to grow spiritually and personally. “Una ang Ginoo” reminds me to walk with humility, faith, and sincerity. It inspires me to live a life that honors God through my words, actions, and choices every day.
